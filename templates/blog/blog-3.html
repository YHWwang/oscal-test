Elevate Your Tablet Experience with Doke OS_P 1.0

Hi guys,

I've got a Tab 9 recently, and am impressed by its self-developed OS, Doke OS_P 1.0. It pleases eyes and delivers a smooth experience. Here I'd like to share with you some of its main features.

Cold Room

Cold Room is used for disabling the stealthy running of rogue apps, saving power and boosting performance. Apps in frozen state can be restored anytime. If your tablet is running slow, its battery starts draining quicker than ever, or the tablet overheats and need to cool down, you can try this function.

System Manager(Via OTA to update)

According to the information released, it is an all-in-one solution and has smart control over trash cleaning, battery performance, gaming boosting, and how apps run in the background. I am looking forward to try this function.

Dark Mode

Dark Mode, which has light text on a dark background, reduces the light emitted by device screens while maintaining the minimum color contrast ratios. The result is reducing eye strain in dim light. It seems helpful for those who stay up late at night.

Reading Mode

Without the interference of color, my tablet is more like an e-reader as Kindle. Actually, to some extent, Reading Mode dampens my urge to to keep loading up social media feeds or mobile games.

Gaussian Blur

It is a nice feature. Highlight the main task window by blurring the background so useful information stands out more clearly. Gaussian Blur applies to the drop-down menu, folders and more.

Smooth gesture operations

Thanks to Doke OS_P 1.0, I have a smooth interact with Tab 9 by performing gestures on the touchscreen. It really enhances the navigation experience and makes me want to interact with my tablet more.

Screenshot and Long screenshot

It is easy to take a screenshot and long screenshot. And users are allowed to edit the screenshot with built-in tools such as Graffiti, mosaic and Free Shot.

Customized Color & Temperature

Tab 9 supports full color temperature management which allows the system to adapt in the overall visual quality. This opens up more ways for us to personalize our device.

Exquisite App Icons and Wallpapers

Exquisite app icons are pleasing to the eye. And several wallpapers are provided for the big screen. Among all of these, the beautiful landscape wallpaper is my favorite.
